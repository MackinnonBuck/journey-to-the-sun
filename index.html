Journey to the Sun

A music video to Parov Stelar's electroswing song "The Sun" that tells the story of an astronaut returning from a distant space station to his comfortable home within the inner solar system.

Story, animation, and rendering by
Keavon Chambers and Mackinnon Buck


Scene Snapshots

Implementation Notes

  • All scenes rendered using C++ with OpenGL
  • Scenes and animations created in Blender
  • Exported animation F-curves with a custom script
  • Interpolated the Bezier F-curves for in-engine rendering
  • PBR shaders written for prettier lighting
  • Bloom post-processing effect for light bleed
  • Particle system for the space dust
  • Billboard animation for the ship's engine plasma trails
